# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 9, 2026**

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Cisco IOS Gemini AI Expert ("App," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our platform.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 User Input Data
- **Cisco CLI Queries**: Commands and technical questions you enter into the application
- **Network Configuration Data**: Any network configuration information, topology details, or device information you provide
- **Images**: Screenshots or images you upload for analysis (CLI output, topology diagrams, etc.)

### 2.2 Voice Input Data
- **Speech Transcripts**: When you use the voice input feature, your speech is converted to text using your browser's Web Speech API
- **Voice recognition data is processed locally in your browser and transmitted only if you choose to submit it

### 2.3 Application Usage Data
- **Chat History**: The App stores your conversation history locally in your browser using localStorage
- **Model Selection**: Your preferred AI model choice
- **Session Data**: Information about your browsing session (dark/light theme preference, etc.)

### 2.4 API Communication Data
When you submit a query, the App sends the following to Google's Gemini AI API:
- Your technical query text
- Any uploaded images
- Your selected AI model preference
- System instruction context for accurate responses

### 2.5 Browser and Device Information
- Standard HTTP request headers
- Browser type and version (as transmitted by your browser)
- Device type (desktop/mobile)

## 3. How We Use Your Information

### 3.1 Primary Purposes
- **Provide Services**: To generate accurate Cisco IOS technical documentation and command analysis
- **Image Analysis**: To analyze uploaded network topology diagrams and CLI output screenshots
- **Model Optimization**: To improve response quality and accuracy
- **Search Enhancement**: To provide research-backed documentation when you enable research mode

### 3.2 AI Processing
Your queries and uploaded content are processed by:
- **Google Gemini API**: To generate technical responses
- **Google Search Integration**: If research mode is enabled, to find official Cisco documentation
- **Google Generative AI Models**: Including Gemini 3 Pro, Gemini 3 Flash, and Gemini Flash Lite

## 4. Data Storage and Retention

### 4.1 Local Storage
- **Chat History**: Stored locally on your device using browser localStorage
- **Suggestions Cache**: Your suggestion preferences are stored locally
- **Automatic Cleanup**: You can clear all local data using the "Clear History" button within the App
- **Browser Dependent**: Data is deleted when you clear your browser cache or use private browsing mode

### 4.2 Google API Servers
- **Temporary Processing**: Your queries are processed temporarily by Google Gemini API servers
- **Retention Policy**: Google maintains its own data retention policies as outlined in Google Cloud's privacy documentation
- **No Long-term Storage**: The App does not store your queries on our servers

### 4.3 No Database Storage
The App does not maintain any backend database. All query history is managed solely through your browser's localStorage.

## 5. Information Sharing

### 5.1 Third-Party Service Providers
We share your information with:
- **Google Cloud**: For AI processing through Gemini API (required for core functionality)
- **Google Search**: When research mode is enabled (optional feature)

### 5.2 Legal Compliance
We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government request.

### 5.3 No Commercial Sharing
- We do not sell your data
- We do not share your technical queries with third parties for marketing purposes
- We do not use your Cisco configuration data for competitive intelligence

## 6. Data Security

### 6.1 Encryption
- **In Transit**: All communications with Google APIs use HTTPS encryption
- **Browser Protection**: Your local data is protected by your browser's security features
- **No Server Encryption**: Since we don't store data on servers, server-side encryption is not applicable

### 6.2 API Key Security
- **Never Transmitted to Frontend**: Your Gemini API key is stored in environment variables and never exposed to the frontend
- **Server-Side Validation**: API requests must be authenticated through secure backend channels
- **No Client-Side Storage**: The API key is not stored in your browser

### 6.3 Best Practices
- Use strong, unique passwords for your Google Cloud accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication on your Google account
- Regularly review your API key access and rotation in Google Cloud Console

## 7. User Rights and Controls

### 7.1 Data Access
- You have full access to your chat history within the App
- You can export your conversation history by copying it from the application interface

### 7.2 Data Deletion
- **Automatic Deletion**: Click the "Clear History" button to permanently delete all locally stored data
- **Browser Clearing**: Clearing your browser cache will delete all App data
- **Upon Request**: Contact us to request deletion of data associated with your queries

### 7.3 Data Portability
- Your chat history can be manually copied or exported from the App interface
